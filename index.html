ABOUT US.

The Offaly Africa Support Group is an Irish non-governmental and voluntary organisation in Offaly, set up by ______ _______. Our main objectives are to facilitate programmes that link the Irish culture with the African culture, heritage, and diversity in Ireland.
We also work with government, non-government bodies, and other minority groups to help access programmes that promote equal opportunities for the African and migrant communities in Offaly.

Our MISSION
Is to work with other organisations and community groups to identify, coordinate, and facilitate programmes that promote integration.

Our VISION
Is to create a well-integrated and inclusive community where everyone is accepted, respected, treated fairly, and welcomed.

To complete our OBJECTIVES
We will collaborate with governmental, non-governmental, and other minority organisations to gain access to initiatives that support fair opportunities for Offaly's African and immigrant populations.

#WeAreBetterTogether.

A big thank you to the Offaly County Council for organising a meeting with the Offaly Africa Support Group in Edenderry. The primary objectives of the meeting were to promote initiatives supporting cultural diversity, inclusion and integration.

The OASG held their monthly meeting at the Tullamore Court Hotel. Several ideas were shared, including organising a public general meeting, forming soccer teams, planning an intercultural day and hosting a Christmas party.

Anyone is welcome to join the OASG. Thanks to the commitee members for their continued support! #WeAreBetterTogether
