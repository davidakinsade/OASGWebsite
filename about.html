#WeAreBetterTogether.

A big thanks to the Offaly County Council for facilitating a productive meeting with the Offaly Africa Support Group (OASG) in Edenderry. This gathering provided a platform to discuss key initiatives focused on promoting cultural diversity and encouraging integration within our community. Such efforts are vital in building stronger connections among people from diverse backgrounds.

In addition to that, the OASG held its regular monthly meeting at the Tullamore Court Hotel. The discussions showcased a variety of ideas to strengthen community engagement and celebrate diversity. Among the proposals were plans to organize a public general meeting to foster participation, form soccer teams to promote teamwork, and host an intercultural day that would celebrate the rich traditions of different African cultures. Additionally, members brainstormed plans for a festive Christmas party.

The OASG warmly welcomes new members to join the group and contribute. Whether you have ideas to share or simply want to connect with others in your community, your involvement will be highly valued. A special thanks goes to the committee members for their efforts in making these projects possible. #WeAreBetterTogether
